## Page Body

### Your System Has a Season Too

An irrigation system that's properly prepared for the seasons performs better, lasts longer, and costs less to maintain. Skipping spring start-up or winterization might seem like a way to save time, but it's a common reason systems fail prematurely.

Sprinkler Medic provides seasonal irrigation services throughout north Alabama — getting your system ready for the heat of summer and protecting it before freezing temperatures arrive.

### Spring Start-Up

When it's time to bring your system back online, we don't just flip a switch and call it done. Our spring start-up includes:

- Turning on the water supply and pressurizing the system
- Checking each zone for coverage, broken heads, and proper operation
- Adjusting head heights and spray patterns as needed
- Inspecting and testing valves for proper open/close function
- Reviewing controller settings and updating the schedule for current conditions
- Checking the backflow preventer and replacing any damaged components
- Documenting any repairs needed

You'll start the irrigation season knowing your system is ready.

### Winterization (Blowout Service)

Water left in irrigation lines can freeze, expand, and crack pipes, heads, and valves — causing expensive damage that won't show up until spring. A proper winterization removes that risk entirely.

Our winterization service includes:

- Controller shutoff and deactivation
- Main water supply shutoff
- Compressed air blowout of all zones — clearing water from every line and head
- Valve inspection and winterization
- Backflow preventer draining and protection
- Final system walkthrough

We use properly sized compressors rated for irrigation systems — not undersized equipment that leaves water behind.

### Why It Matters in Northern Alabama

While north Alabama doesn't see the extreme winters of further north states, hard freezes still happen — and they happen quickly. One overnight freeze with water still in your lines is all it takes. It's a small service that prevents a large repair bill.
