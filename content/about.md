## Company Story

**Headline:**
Our Story

**Body:**
Sprinkler Medic started with a straightforward premise: north Alabama homeowners deserved a reliable, knowledgeable irrigation company that treated them like neighbors — not customers on a ticket queue.

Owner Josh Gamble has spent over a decade mastering the craft of irrigation. He started Sprinkler Medic because he saw a gap in the market: too many contractors cutting corners, too few who actually cared about the long-term health of a client's system and yard.

Eleven years later, that commitment hasn't changed. Josh and his team have installed and serviced hundreds of systems across the Huntsville metro area, from simple backyard repairs to full commercial-grade installations. Every job gets the same attention — whether it's a single broken sprinkler head or a complete new system from the ground up.

We're based in New Market, and we work across Huntsville, Madison, Gurley, Decatur, Athens, Toney, Hazel Green, Owens Cross Road, and surrounding communities. This is our home. We plan to be here for the next decade too.
