## Services Overview Section

**Section Headline:**
What We Do

**Section Subheadline:**
From emergency repairs to full system installations, Sprinkler Medic handles every aspect of your irrigation and outdoor systems.

### Service Cards

**Irrigation Service & Repair**
Your sprinkler system not working right? We diagnose and fix broken heads, faulty valves, controller issues, and everything in between. Fast turnaround — most repairs done same day.

**Irrigation Installation**
Planning a new system or expanding existing coverage? We design and install efficient irrigation systems built for your lawn's specific layout and your water goals.

**Leak Detection**
Unexplained water bills? Soggy spots in the yard? We track down hidden leaks quickly and fix them before small problems become expensive ones.

**Spring Start-Up & Winterization**
Protect your investment. We get your system ready for the season in spring and properly shut it down before the first freeze. No stress, no surprises.

**Outdoor Lighting**
Extend the beauty of your landscape into the evening. We design and install landscape lighting systems that highlight your property and add security.

**Drainage**
Standing water ruins lawns and damages foundations. We install French drains, catch basins, and grading solutions to direct water where it belongs.
